Project Risk Assessment Agent

Early-Stage Risk Identification for Construction, Manufacturing & IT Projects

Research shows that ~70% of project failures stem from decisions made in the first 20% of the project lifecycle — before major commitments become physically irreversible. This tool helps you identify, prioritise, and act on risks before it is too late.

🚀 Start Assessment

What this tool does

📋

Risk Register

Generates a scored and sorted risk register combining your top concerns with industry-specific knowledge-base risks.

🧰

Decision Frameworks

Selects contextually relevant decision frameworks from the thesis — Safety Premium, Somatic Verification, Bureaucratic Shield, and more.

📅

20% Reality Check

Provides a structured workshop agenda for your mandatory mid-phase risk-validation milestone.

🌍

Cultural Archetypes

Detects your cultural risk archetype (Process Guardian / Resource Navigator) and provides cross-training recommendations.

📄

PDF Export

Download your full report as a professionally formatted PDF — ready to share with your sponsor and team.

🎯

Personalised Guidance

Tailored strengths, development areas, and recommended actions based on your experience level (Junior / Mid / Senior).
